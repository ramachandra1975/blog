Who I Am

A mind that refuses one discipline

Over 25 years of lived experience crossing Vedic literature, Indian classical performing arts, financial audit, corporate operations, and digital media — I inhabit the unexplored intersections that specialists overlook.

My work as a researcher focuses on Natya Shastra, Nada Chikitsa (sound as medicine), and consciousness studies. As a writer, I translate Sanskrit texts for practising scholars, craft long-form research content, and ghost-write for thinkers who need words worthy of their ideas. As a web architect, I have built one of the internet's most comprehensive platforms for applied Vedic knowledge — 26 research subdomains, AI integration, and a 50+ source curated archive.

Age is not a limitation. Breadth is the point.
